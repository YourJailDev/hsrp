Honolulu Fire Department

The Honolulu Fire Department is a department which is specialized in handling calls related to fire or medical injuries. It consists for 2 divisions. They are the Fire Fighters and the Emergency Medical Service (EMS). Fire Fighters Division are only to handle the fire related calls, for example a house that is on fire, a forest that is on fire, a car/building on fire, etc. Whilst the Emergency Medical Service only handles calls that are medical related, for example a broken ankle, a revival request, etc. Fire Fighters Division consists of Firefighters, while Emergency Medical Service Division consists of Paramedics.

Honolulu Police Department

"Protect and Serve" The Honolulu Police Department is a law enforcement department responsible for the city and it's residents safety. We handle day-to-day issues such as traffic violations, warrant services, and general public protection. While regular officers are restricted to cadet-only gear, trained HPD officers have full access to all non-gamepass gear. We have a specialized team that utilizes extensive training and respond to high risk calls, our Special Weapons and Tactics team is given the very best gear and equipment to handle the worst possible scenarios. The HPD also contains a Detective/Undercover unit tasked and trained with carrying out operations to apprehend our most wanted criminals.
